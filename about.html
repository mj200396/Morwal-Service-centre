Our Story

Founded in 2009 by Mr. Rajesh Morwal, our service centre began as a small workshop with a simple vision: to provide honest, reliable, and professional two-wheeler maintenance services to the people of Kota.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into a full-fledged service centre with a team of expert mechanics, modern equipment, and a loyal customer base of over 5,000 satisfied vehicle owners.

Today, we continue to uphold our founding principles of transparency, quality, and customer-first approach, making us the most trusted name in two-wheeler service in Kota.
